═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
.env (frontend) — dipakai juga di Cloudflare Pages:
- VITE_SUPABASE_URL
- VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
- VITE_APPS_SCRIPT_URL          ← required (SOP + upload foto)
- VITE_DRIVE_FOLDER_ID          ← upload foto folder
- VITE_GOOGLE_API_KEY           ← KPI Sheets
- VITE_GOOGLE_SOP_FOLDER_ID     ← (kalau Drive direct fallback diaktifkan)
- VITE_GOOGLE_KPI_SHEET_ID      ← KPI sheet
- VITE_STAFF_PASS               ← shared password staff
- VITE_BACKEND_URL              ← OPSIONAL, isi cuma kalau backend deploy
- SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY     ← buat scripts/* lokal saja, JANGAN ke Cloudflare
